Information Visualization

I analyzed NJ Transit rail arrival times at New York Penn Station, and created two visualizations to show how likely the train is to be late and the frequency & time late depending on hour & day. Data was cleaned and processed using Python. Visualizations were created using the D3 Javascript library.

Machine Learning

My goal for this project was to try to predict genres using review data. I tried to predict what genres Asian television dramas were labeled as, based on user review text. I ran the text through both TF-IDF and word2vec, and then through a mix of classifiers: logistic regression, random forest, and naive Bayes. In order to account for differences in the amount of data available for different genres, data was selectively over- and undersampled. Done in Python.
